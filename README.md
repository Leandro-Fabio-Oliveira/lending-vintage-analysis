# Credit Portfolio Risk Analytics
## Vintage Analysis & Delinquency Segmentation


## Business Problem
Lenders need to monitor loan portfolio health over time — not just at
origination, but as loans age. A portfolio can look healthy today and show
stress 6 months later. Vintage analysis solves this by tracking cohorts of
loans originated in the same period, allowing risk teams to detect early
signals of deterioration and adjust credt policies proactively.

## Dataset

• Source: [LendingClub public loan data](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/wordsforthewise/lending-club/data)
(2007 - 2018)
• ~2.2 million accepted loan records

## Methodology
1. Data Preparation
2. Exploratory Data Analysis
3. Vintage Analysis — deliquency curves by origination cohort
4. Deliquancy Segmentation — wich borrower profiles underperformed across vintages
5. Predictive Model — early warning classifier for default in the first 6 months
